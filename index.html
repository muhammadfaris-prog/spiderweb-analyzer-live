ETAC 2024 Programme Learning Outcomes (PLOs)

  • PO1: Knowledge - Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and engineering technology principles.
  • PO2: Problem Analysis - Identify, formulate, and solve engineering technology problems.
  • PO3: Design/Development of Solutions - Design solutions for engineering technology problems and systems with appropriate consideration for safety, environment, and sustainability.
  • PO4: Investigation - Conduct investigations into complex problems using research-based knowledge and methods.
  • PO5: Tool Usage - Select and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering tools.
  • PO6: The Engineering Technologist and the World - Apply reasoning informed by contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues.
  • PO7: Ethics - Understand and commit to professional ethics, responsibilities, and norms of engineering technology practice.
  • PO8: Individual and Collaborative Team Work - Function effectively as an individual and in diverse teams.
  • PO9: Communications - Communicate effectively on engineering technology activities with the community and society.
  • PO10: Project Management and Finance - Demonstrate knowledge of project management principles and economic decision-making.
  • PO11: Life Long Learning - Recognize the need for, and engage in, independent and life-long learning.
